Public Transport

In Melbourne forms of Public Transports are common, it is important to get a MyKi card and top it up in order to use them.

Tram

In melbourne, trams are a common form of public transport. La Trobe University has a tram stop on route 86 for anyone looking to use the tram to reach the venue. PTV has an app available to download on IOS and Android for users to enter La Trobe University and have a route planned ahead for them.

Train

Melbourne has a huge network of train stations, make sure to get an image of the train map to find the train stations near you. However, La Trobe does not have a train station nearby and is required for you to take a train station to the nearest available public transport that reaches La Trobe. PTV has an app available to download on IOS and Android for users to enter La Trobe University and have a route planned ahead for them.

Bus

Busses are a common form of transport in Melbourne, it is available in most places of the area and has a Bus stop in La Trobe University. There are many different routes of busses that goes through La Trobe Bus stop so be sue to get the PTV app for IOS and Android users that would plan the route to La Trobe University for you.
