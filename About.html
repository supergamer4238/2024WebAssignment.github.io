About Us

The Annual La Trobe University Conference has been going on since 2015. The conference aim to provide everyone with an insight of working in IT and Computer Science industry. If you are already experienced in the field there will be invited speakers that will go in detail on the field. Otherwise you can find your interest here in La Trobe University Annual conference.

Partners

The conference is organized by La Trobe University's Department of Computer Science and IT, in collaboration with several industry partners and sponsors. Our partners include Monash University, The University of Melbourne, Swinburne University and more. We aim to partner with more communities and organisations to be a inclusive community allow us to network and make friends with one another.
